## Status vocabulary

- **OPEN** — not yet proved.
- **HALF** — some real work exists. The missing proof is named.
- **PASS** — the named phase outcome is freshly proved. It is not a whole-product claim.
- **FAIL** — the evidence disproves the phase claim or the test gate is red.

No phase may replace its verdict with prose such as “mostly done,” “should work,” or “green historically.”
