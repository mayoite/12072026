1. **Gate folders go green** (W1–W8, CP-00…09) → language drifts toward “done.”

3. **Owner uses the product** and correctly feels “this is still a 2.”  
4. Engineering then argues evidence paths. Owner is measuring **buyer feel**. Those are different meters.

`ayushdocs/04-HONEST-QUALITY.md` already said it: hard path is a **real spine**, not a finished product; rough **~40–50%** asset-engine skeleton, **~20–30%** production place-good-mesh. That split is why unit-green can feel like a lie without any single test being false.

**Owner disappointment is valid.** Not a communication failure on the owner side. The product is still short of “acceptable public product” (3) on almost every dim that matters for a manufacturer planner.
