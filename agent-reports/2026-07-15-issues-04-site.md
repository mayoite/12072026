# Issues 04 — Site (public)

**Source:** Earlier major-issue audit + `plan/Site/CHECKLIST.md` + FEATURES.  
**UI verified:** No.  
**One sentence each.**

1. Full funnel is not measurable in production because `PAGE_VIEW` and most downstream conversion events are unwired.  
2. Consent gating before analytics emit is incomplete.  
3. Source and campaign context does not reliably survive Site to Planner entry.  
4. Production-like `window.va` event receipt is not verified.  
5. Funnel, source, failure, and page reporting dashboards do not exist as acceptance evidence.  
6. Sitemap and robots are hand-maintained and disagree with `routeClassification` indexability rules.  
7. Field Core Web Vitals budgets are not recorded or met.  
8. Critical console, request, and hydration failures lack a fresh production-like run sign-off.  
9. Customer segments, pages, and nav are not mapped to one intent and next action.  
10. Form acceptance including consent remains open.  
11. Mobile commercial benchmarks remain open.  
12. A11y smoke is not WCAG 2.2 AA sign-off for primary journeys.  
13. Most page CTAs do not fire planner entry conversion beyond header wiring.  
14. Search-led content has no search-evidence workflow, ownership, or review dates.  
15. Public product discovery can use fixture fallback when Products DB is unset.  
16. Product detail always presents stock-like availability without a true released-stock contract.  
17. Product and family identity does not hand off cleanly into Planner.  
18. Phone product filters and comparison are incomplete.  
19. Structured data is not validated against visible released content.  
20. Full discovery to Planner to handoff journey is not rechecked under production-like build with monitoring.
