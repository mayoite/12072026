## Your Questions Answered

### What can I do with a Free subscription?

With RoomSketcher Free, you can create floor plan projects, draw and furnish rooms, and try basic 3D viewing. Some outputs and advanced features, such as high-quality floor plans, professional customization, and certain 3D features, require an upgrade or paid add-on.

### Which RoomSketcher subscription is right for me?

Choose **Free** if you want to try RoomSketcher and create simple projects.

Choose **Pro** if you want professional 2D and 3D Floor Plans, interactive Live 3D walkthroughs, measurements, customization, and branding.

Choose **Team** if you need multiple users, higher volume, or several floor plan styles.

### Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. Monthly and annual RoomSketcher subscriptions can be canceled anytime. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period.

### Can AI create my floor plan?

Yes. RoomSketcher’s AI Convert can turn a computer-generated blueprint into an editable floor plan. The AI detects walls, doors, and windows, and you can then refine the result in the RoomSketcher App.

### Can I use AI Convert with a Free subscription?

Yes. AI Convert is available for Free, Pro, and Team users through the web portal, so you can try converting a blueprint into an editable RoomSketcher project. And your first AI Convert is free!

### Can RoomSketcher draw the floor plan for me?

Yes. You can upload a blueprint or sketch and order professional floor plans from RoomSketcher. The finished project is delivered to your account, where you can view, download, print, or edit it.

### Can I download, print, or export my floor plans?

Yes. RoomSketcher lets you download and print your 2D and 3D Floor Plans in different formats and layouts.

### Can I use RoomSketcher in my browser?

Yes. You can use the RoomSketcher web portal in your browser to start and manage projects, upload blueprints, use AI Convert, place floor plan orders, generate floor plans, and download your finished plans. To draw or edit floor plans yourself, use the RoomSketcher App on a computer or tablet.
