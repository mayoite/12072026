## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is a floor plan and why are they important?

A floor plan is a scaled diagram of a room or building viewed from above, showing the relationships between rooms, spaces, and physical features.

They are important because they clearly visualize a space, which helps plan, design, and communicate ideas effectively.

### What are the different types of floor plans?

There are two main types: 2D and 3D floor plans.

A **2D floor plan** is a flat, top-down representation of a space, showing layout, walls, doors, and furniture without depth or perspective. A **3D floor plan**, on the other hand, adds height and depth, providing a more realistic visualization of the space with textures, lighting, and a sense of scale.

RoomSketcher allows you to create both 2D and 3D Floor Plans, providing different perspectives for your projects.
