## How Do I Find a Door?

You can find doors under **Window etc**. mode in the RoomSketcher App. Here’s how:

1. Click or tap the red **Mode** button and open **Windows etc**. mode.
2. Open the **Doors** category.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466925)

### What Kind of Doors Can I Find?

- You’ll see a variety of door types, listed alphabetically. These include bi-fold, exterior, french, glass, interior, pocket, sliding, and even a door and wall opening (a walk-through space with no actual door).
- Pro and Team subscribers have access to trim less **door models without thresholds**, shown slightly open in the library so you can easily distinguish them from standard doors.

**Tips**:

- Hover over any door to view its default size.
- For the best rendering results, choose a door that’s close to the size you need.


## How Do I Add a Door?

Once you’ve found the door you want, it just takes a few clicks to place it:

1. Click to select a door.
2. Click on a wall to place the door.

**Note**: The door's length must not exceed the wall's length. If the door is wider than the wall's length, it can't be added to the wall.

## How Can I Change the Size, Opening Angle, and Swing of the Door?

You can adjust how a door fits and functions in your floor plan using the door’s properties. Whether you want to fine-tune the size, swing direction, or even close a door completely, RoomSketcher gives you direct control to make these edits quickly.

1. With a door selected, its **Properties** appear on the right.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001460909)

2. To change the direction of the swing, click **Flip Door** or use the hotkey **Q**.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001460929)



**Tip**: If _Flip Door_ is greyed out, the door is locked. To unlock a door, scroll down to **Advanced Properties** and toggle **Lock** off.

3. To change the size, drag the blue arrows, or in the field **Width**, enter the correct width of the door. In **Height**, adjust the height of the door.

All sizes include trim.

4. For **Opening Angle**, type a number in degrees to adjust the swing angle of the door.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466785)





**Tip:** If you want a closed door, type **0** for the angle.

### How Can I Change the Color or Materials of the Door?

If you have a Premium Project, a Pro, or a Team subscription and see this symbol ![mceclip0.png](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/6955885796509) on a door, you can change the color and materials.

1. **Select** a door you placed in your project so that its **Properties** appear on the right.
2. Under **Replace Materials**, you’ll see the door's parts listed. You can typically change the handle, door, sill, or trim materials.

Some doors (such as exterior doors) can have a different color on each side. Side A is the side with hinges.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466825)

3. Click the name of a door part.
4. The **Select Material** page appears. You can:

\-  Use the scroll bar to browse suggested materials, or

\-  In **Search**, type a word, such as “Wood”, “Paint” or “Metal”, to narrow your selection.

\-  To use a custom color, click the **Paint Palette**.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466845)



    \- Type a hex or RGB color code, or click the color wheel to select a color.



![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466865)

5. Once you have chosen a material, click **Apply** to close the **Select Material** page.

## How Do I View the Door in My Project?

To visualize your custom door, click and drag the camera to point it at the door, then double-click to take a [snapshot](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/en-us/articles/208846525).

![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466885)

## How Can I Copy, Move, or Delete a Door?

- **Copy:** Click a door to select it, and click the **Copy**![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001466905) button in the top toolbar. Then click a Wall and click the **Paste**![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001460949) button.
- **Move:** You can drag a door to move it.
- **Delete:** Click the **Trash Bin**![](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/360001460969) to delete it.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Can I change the direction a door opens?**

Yes. You can adjust the swing direction of a door by selecting
it and using the rotate or flip options.

**How can I add barn doors?**

You can add a barn door by placing a door opening onto your
wall and then add a barn door from the furniture library.
You can find detailed steps here:
[How Can I Add Barn Doors to My Floor Plans?](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/en-us/articles/22453135036573-How-Can-I-Add-Barn-Doors-to-My-Floor-Plans)

**Can I show a bifold door as open?**

It's currently not possible to show bifold doors
as open. You can use a standard single- or double-door
to visualize opening angles.
