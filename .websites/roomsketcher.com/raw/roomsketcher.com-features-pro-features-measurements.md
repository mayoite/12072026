# Add Measurements to Your Floor Plans

Make your floor plans more professional by adding accurate measurements that show size, layout, and space.

[Get Started](https://www.roomsketcher.com/get-started/)

## Why Measurements Matter

Accurate measurements make your floor plans more useful. They help clients and teams understand the space, make faster decisions, and trust your work.

Whether you're designing, presenting, or selling, precise measurements add confidence and value to every project.

Watch the video or keep reading to learn how to use measurements in the RoomSketcher app.

## Show The Area of a Room

Easily display the usable area of each room directly on your floor plan, so it's clear how much space is available. This measurement updates automatically whenever you adjust the layout, making sure your floor plan always reflects the true size of the space.

## Add Room Dimensions (Length x Width)

Show room dimensions in feet and inches, or meters and centimeters. Or show both units in each room! With the automatic Measurement Wizards, just click in each room and the correct measurements magically appear. Want to edit the placement? Just click and drag to move the label.

## Show Inside, Outside, and One Wall Measurements

Accurately show the length of any wall, on the inside or outside of your floor plan. Inside wall measurements help with layout and furnishing, and outside wall lengths make it easy to understand the total structure size. You can display every wall in a room at once or select individual walls to show just what matters.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I show measurements in feet and inches or in meters?

RoomSketcher lets you choose your preferred unit system. You can display measurements in either imperial (feet/inches) or metric (meters/centimeters).

### Can I choose which measurements are visible on my floor plan?

Yes! You can show or hide room dimensions, wall lengths, room areas, and more. You have full control over what’s displayed.

### How do I add dimensions to a floor plan in RoomSketcher?

You can use the Measurement Wizards to quickly show room dimensions (Length x Width).

### Can I measure the total area of a floor plan?

Yes! RoomSketcher automatically calculates the area of each room as you draw. You can also calculate the total area using options like GFA, GIA, or GTA — or define your own.

### What scale should I use for a floor plan?

RoomSketcher supports standard architectural scales, such as 1:50 and 1:100, when saving floor plans as PDFs. You can choose the best scale based on your paper size and project needs.

### What are typical floor plan dimensions?

Typical dimensions vary depending on the type of property. With RoomSketcher, you define the size of every room — so your floor plan can match anything from a studio to a large commercial space.

### How do I show specific dimensions, like between furniture?

Use the measuring tape tool to measure the distance between any two points. It’s great for checking clearances and planning layouts.

### How accurate are the measurements in RoomSketcher?

As long as your plan is drawn to scale, RoomSketcher provides accurate, real-time measurements that update automatically when you make changes to the layout.
