## Where Do I Find the Tape Measure?

You can show or hide the Tape Measure. To open it, click or tap **Menu**(the yellow button at the top left) and press **Tape Measure**. You can be in any mode (for example, in Furniture mode).

![menu_-_tape_measure.jpg](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/8253696744477)

## How Do I Move and Size the Tape Measure?

**Here's how you move or rotate the Tape Measure:**

- Press and drag the center of the Tape Measure to move it.
- Press and drag an end to rotate it.

**Tip:** As you rotate the Tape Measure, it snaps in place at 45 degree increments. To turn snapping ON or OFF, open the **Tools** menu and press **Snapping**.

**Here's how you change the size of the Tape Measure:**

- Press and drag the arrows at either end of the Tape Measure to size it. As you adjust the size, the length between the cross-hairs displays.
- Tip: To change the measurement system, press **Menu** and under Tools, click **Meters** or **Feet**.

Top tip: It can be easier to use the Tape Measure after you have zoomed in closer on the drawing pane. Learn how you do so here: [How can I Zoom In and Out on a Drawing?](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/en-us/articles/360018185018)

## Can I Lock the Tape Measure?

Yes, you can lock the Tape Measure so that it can't be accidentally moved or re-sized. Press the **Lock** or **Unlock** button near the top toolbar.

![Ma_band_2.png](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/article_attachments/21082504827677)

## Here Are RoomSketcher's Top Usage Tips

Place an item of furniture a certain distance from a wall:

- In **Walls** mode, draw your walls. Place the **Tape Measure** near a wall and size it to the distance the furniture should be placed away from the wall. Switch to **Furniture** mode and add that bed or sofa.

Place a wall a certain distance from an item of furniture:

- In **Furniture** mode, place an item of furniture. Place the **Tape Measure** along the edge of the furniture and adjust the Tape Measure’s length to the distance the wall should be away from the furniture. Switch to **Walls** mode and add the wall the correct distance away.

Show measurements on your 2D Floor Plans

- The **Tape Measure** won't show on generated floor plans. If you want to display measurements on your floor plans, choose one of the options shown here: [Overview: What Measurements Can I Add to My Floor Plans?](https://help.roomsketcher.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000216525)
