## [AI-Enhanced Exports & AI Tokens](https://updates.floorplanner.com/ai-enhanced-exports-ai-tokens-323395)

We're launching two connected features: AI image enhancement and AI tokens (for granular pricing)

**Enhance with AI**

Transform any export (3D interiors, 2D/3D floorplans) by adding your own touch. Change styles, add/remove objects, modify materials and backgrounds, or incorporate your own images—logos, actual window views, even photos of yourself or your pet.

![2025-09-18 14.21.38.gif](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/149/027-f90d9dbf2e6893c6bc6d60dd1bc131d0b226923d.gif)

How to use this new feature:

- Go to Exports tab → select any export
- Click "Enhance with AI" in the left sidebar
- Write your enhancement prompt or choose from defaults
- Upload additional images if needed
- Click on the "Enhance image" button (20 AI Tokens needed)

Note: The results might vary and always carefully check the resulting images for accuracy before sharing them with other.

**AI Tokens: A New Micro Currency**

Since AI processing has real costs, we created a new micro currency:AI tokens for affordable per-use pricing instead of charging full credits.

- Cost: 20 AI tokens per image (5 images = 1 credit)

- Get tokens: Convert 1 credit → 100 AI tokens via dashboard


Important: AI tokens don't expire but can't be converted back to credits

All paid accounts (PLUS, PRO, and higher) have some starter AI tokens to test the feature before converting additional credits. Sub-users of Teams accounts and higher do not have access to this feature yet. This will soon be added, for now only the main users of an account can use this feature.

## [Big December Update for Basic Accounts](https://updates.floorplanner.com/big-december-update-for-basic-accounts-305535)

We are adding some great new features to our Basic (Free) accounts and are making some changes to the pricing structure of our platform to ensure we can keep a great free product for all our users.

![Screenshot 2024-12-02 at 14.19.03.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/137/241-167cd57ddae5bd6b8b32ba728202ff3362506e58.png)

We are extending our favorite & group features to ALL our users. With a Basic (free) account you can now save up to 20 favourites and up to 5 custom groups that you can easily reuse in your projects. If you upgrade to any paid plan you can now even store as much favorites & groups as you want.

Besides these free new features, we are also putting a limitation in place. From now on, every account is limited to 5 Free projects (SD-Lite). You can create as many projects as you want, but every new project after the first 5 needs to be paid for with credits.These Free projects, however, have more functionality than our previous Free projects, as you can create up to 3 floors within the same project now. In case you have more projects in your account, no worries; they will still be fully accessible.

We feel 5 projects with 3 floors should be enough for pretty much every casual user, and if you are using Floorplanner professionally, we think you'll be better served via one of our reasonably priced paid plans anyway.

To make it more attractive to upgrade to one of our paid plans, we have a number of deals that will only be available during the coming month. You can find these within your account.

## [Groups!](https://updates.floorplanner.com/groups!-305441)

You can now create Groups with every Floorplanner account. A group is a collection of objects that you can store and re-use within any of your projects. For instance a fully filled bookcase or a set of 4 desks with monitors & chairs.

Groups can be created within the editor by selecting multiple objects (via shift-select or manually clicking one-by-one) and click on the "Create Group" button. A new tab with "groups" will appear in you Object library where you can find all your saved groups and you can simply drag them in your plan. Basic users can store up to 5 groups within a Free account and with a paid account (PLUS and higher) you can save as much groups as you need!

An introduction of the [Group Feature](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OQMsIlFxp9o) can be found on Youtube
