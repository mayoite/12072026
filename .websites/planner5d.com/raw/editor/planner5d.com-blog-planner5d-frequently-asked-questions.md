## **How to create a render in Planner 5D?**

Rendering is the process of transforming a design into a realistic image. In Planner 5D:

1. Finish decorating the room.
2. Click on the camera icon in the top bar (web version) or in the top right corner (app).
3. Choose the aspect ratio and image quality (HD, Full HD, Quad HD, or 4K, available on paid plans).
4. Wait a few moments for the image to be ready.
