## Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Upon registration, you will be automatically placed on the Free plan. This plan is intended for users who want to get familiar with the platform and create their first basic layout. To create more advanced interior design projects, we recommend choosing the Premium or Professional plan. These plans offer access to a range of professional tools and features that can greatly enhance your project development experience.

How to upgrade?

When you need a Premium or Professional version, visit this page and select a suitable plan based on the features you require. Please note that the annual subscription is significantly cheaper when calculated on a monthly basis. If you want to upgrade from the Premium plan to the Professional plan, please contact our support service.

Do you have special offers for educational institutions?

Especially for educational institutions, we have developed a free version with an extended privacy setting for educational institutions. Visit [this page](https://planner5d.com/education) for detailed information.

What form of payments do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards. If you need an invoice - make sure to enable a toggle on the payment window. If you want to purchase Enterprise solution, [contact us](https://planner5d.com/business#contact_us), and we will issue an invoice that you can pay via bank transfer.
